Kia Stinger 2022

The Stinger from Kia is a sports sedan with a sophisticated design that competes with other cars of the same class, in the attached table we mention the dimensions of the Stinger 2022

Price: 2,450,000 $

Car Engine

A 2.5-liter four-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine exclusive to the North American market, with 300 horsepower at 5,800 rpm and 311 lb-ft (422 Nm) of torque of 1,650 rpm. Genesis G80 3.3L twin-turbo V6 engine comes with Kia Stinger GT 2021 and you'll get a little more power than before, the engine itself has been moved.

The car from the outside

At the front of the updated 2022 Kia Stinger, Kia's distinctive "tiger nose" grille is located among the new multifaceted LED reflective headlights. New design and LED daytime running lights The new lamps look darker and more elegant as the lights are turned off, while giving the car a high-tech light signature, with sharp lines of light pointing slightly towards the front of the car. On the back, the sleek new taillight showcases a new and distinctive light signature.

Car Interior

The Stinger 2022 offers a hugely comfortable rear seat compared to many cars of the same class. The cabin features sleek, durable leather upholstery and Nappa leather seats. Ventilation and articulated improvement are available for heated front seats and heated surfaces for rear passengers. The heated steering wheel option is integrated with all-wheel drive for all trim levels.
