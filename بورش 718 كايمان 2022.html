Car Engine

Primary Engine: The base engine offers a 2.0-liter capacity and is available with a manual transmission and these are its most important features: Generates horsepower (300 HP) at 6500 rpm. Torque reaches 380 Nm at 1950-4500 rpm. Acceleration from 0- 100 km is 4,9 seconds. Fuel consumption is 7.4 l/100 km. Other Porsche 718 engines are available as follows: Engine with a capacity of (2500 cc) generates 350 horsepower and torque 419 Nm.

This car is one of the unique cars in this category, as there are LED headlights and (LED DRLs 4), while the rear lamps are 3D LED. There are also 18, 19 and 20-inch sports wheels, while the distinctive rear spoiler stands out and is integrated with the exterior color, which increases the beauty and elegance of the exterior, and there are electric side mirrors folding, and are offered with tinted rear glass (Famia)
