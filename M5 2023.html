Bmw M5 2023

The BMW M5 2023 has a design that expresses elegance, luxury and sport. The M5 base model comes from the front with a black front kidney grille surrounded by a chrome frame, and the model is equipped with 19-inch light-alloy wheels with a double-spoke design that is metallic

Price: 1,500,000 $

Car Engine

The BMW M5 2023 is powered by a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine with 600 hp or 625 hp (for the M5 competition) with 750 Nm of torque, and the engine is connected to an 8-speed automatic transmission and xDrive rear-wheel drive. Acceleration ranges from 0 to 100 km/h between 3.4 seconds (M5) and 3.3 seconds (M5 competition) and a top speed of about 305 km/h.

The car from the outside

The BMW M5 2023 has a design that expresses elegance, luxury and sport. The M5 base model comes from the front with a black front kidney grille surrounded by a chrome frame, and the model is equipped with 19-inch light-alloy wheels with a double-spoke design in metallic color, and is available in different designs, and in black as well. The M5 model is also available with 20-inch wheels.

Car Interior

The BMW interior features fully Merino leather seats, and the cabin is highly driver-centric in the BMW M5 2023 thanks to new controls that permeate the driver's door, and details from the M Sport inspired by the sports experiences on the track, including the M leather steering wheel with M1 and M2 driver position buttons

Technology and entertainment

Technological and entertainment features of the BMW M5 2023 include a 12.3-inch infotainment screen, head-level display, 3D cockpit, Bluetooth, Mirror Link, Apple Car Play, Android Auto, and AM/FM radio. The technology also includes a standard 600W subwoofer and a 16-speaker Harman Kardon sound system.
