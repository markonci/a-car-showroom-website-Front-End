kia k5 2023

Many distinctive capabilities that carry strong performance and advanced dynamics, making it one of the distinctive sedans provided by the Korean brand Kia

Price: 1,500,000 $

Car Engine

The car is powered by front-wheel drive, a 2-liter four-cylinder engine that generates 152 horsepower, and the engine is mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. The car is powered by gasoline and the fuel consumption rate is estimated at 16.1 km/l

The car from the outside

The Kia K5 2023 is built on the newly developed N3 platform, and the chassis has been redesigned to provide the K5 with a smooth ride and responsive handling, and the sedan carries a distinctive dynamic composition that enters into the exterior design, which came in the form of a set of elements that include the following: Dynamic profile Rear lamp with extended horizontal strip Powerful 18 or 17-inch wheels Built-in camera: 360¬∞ power windows Electric side mirrors

Car Interior

The interior of the Kia K5 K5 2023 is spacious and good, and the car comes with five leather sports seats linked to an electric control system, and an automatic or manual air conditioning system (depending on the categories), in addition to providing the car with several design elements.
