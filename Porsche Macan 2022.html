Car Engine

Engine The design of this car came in ideal dimensions that include a comfortable place for passengers. The Porsche Macan is classified as a rather compact SUV, but this makes it more practical and lighter while driving while maintaining engine performance. This car comes with a twin-turbo V6 engine with a seven-speed dual-grip automatic transmission with all-wheel drive and a capacity of 2.9 liters. It gives up to 434 horsepower.

The car from the outside

The exterior design is one of the most important strengths of the new Macan 2022, as the German company Porsche has struggled a lot to reach the perfect shape of the SUV without affecting performance. The Macan is a wonderful crossover that has many amazing exterior specifications, including: The front end includes built-in body-colored air intakes. The back has LED lights. The side panels have a three-dimensional structured element.

Car Interior

The interior design of this car focuses mainly on the comfort of the driver, and therefore can be considered more comfortable for the front seat passenger as well. Macan is considered an SUV for this reason it pays attention to the presence of certain entertainment specifications, including: 10.9-inch touch screen with user-friendly interface. Redesigned center console with tactile elements and a standard clock.

Technology and entertainment

Special attention has been paid to the safety and security tests of this car, and it has been shown that there are many factors that greatly support driver safety. The main ones are: Rear view camera. Front and rear parking sensors. Lane departure warning. Adaptive cruise control. Forward collision warning. Automatic forward emergency braking system. Blind spot monitoring. Lane change assistant.
